What to Know Before Attending

Over 50,000 items are available at our monthly sales, including used books, records, videos, magazines, and more! Prices range from $0.25 to $1. See below for more!

General Sale Overview

The book sales are hosted on the second Saturday and Sunday of the month. We have 3 rooms available for browsing: the Main Room, the Children's Room and the Bargain Room. Hardcover books sell for $1, while paperbacks sell at $0.50. Children's books from the Bargain room are just $0.25. On Sundays, all books in the bargain room are 50% off.

Gift certificates may also be purchased at $5 denominations.

100% of sales made go to the Palo Alto Library!

Directions

We are located at the Cubberley Community Center! The address is 4000 Middlefield Road, Palo Alto, CA

Our Main Room is located adjacent to the Charleston Shopping Center, along the north driveway.

Safety

For everyone's safety and well-being, we only allow 160 people in the Main Room at a time. On Saturdays, the order is determined by the ticket(s) you receive. These tickets are given out between 8AM and 11AM, which is when the Main Room is opened. Make sure to arrive closer to 8AM to ensure an earlier spot in line. After receiving your tickets, you will be able to leave to browse the rest of the sale or get coffee. Please return to the line prior to your number being called, or you will forfeit your spot.

Upon entering the Main Room, customers are permitted to purchase up to 12 books. Following this, customers should make their way to the east door to make their purchases. If you wish to buy more, you must rejoin the line, using your second ticket. This limit will continue past noon, if a line remains outside.

We accept donations from individuals, estates, and companies!
